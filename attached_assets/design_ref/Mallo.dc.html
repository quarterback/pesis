What is Mallo?

The first analytics site for pesäpallo. English here, Finnish everywhere else.

Public analytics reshaped how baseball and basketball are understood — pesäpallo, a sport with a century of history and a results service that records every plate turn, never had that layer. Mallo is an attempt to build one.

TAHKO is a daily-updating estimate of every player's true talent in every rate stat. Every game a player has played counts, weighted by an exponential decay — no arbitrary "last 10 games" windows — blended with the league average in proportion to the evidence.

TEHO+ is league-indexed production per plate turn (100 = league average, 150 = MVP season), in the spirit of OPS+ / wRC+, so seasons and eras compare honestly.

Percentile profiles show where a player ranks among qualified peers, Baseball Savant style. Kenttäkertoimet are the sport's first published park factors. The roadmap lives in docs/design.md.
